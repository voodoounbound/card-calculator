Card Calculator

Using the Calculator

Pick a card, its rarity, current level, and desired level. Add anything you already have — owned copies, trade tokens, crystals, wild cards — and the calculator subtracts them from the total. Every calculation is saved to the Summary panel on the right, where you can pin favorites, export to CSV, or push a result straight into your Inventory.

Using the Inventory

Track every card's level and owned copies here — they're saved automatically. Log your Gold, Trade Tokens, Wild Cards, Crystals, and Hourglass Tokens in the Resources sidebar. Tick the checkbox on any card to add it to a combined Upgrade Plan, or tap the calculator icon on a card to jump straight into a detailed calculation for just that one. Use the filter, sort, and group icons above the grid to organize things your way.
